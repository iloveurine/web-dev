APU Neon Weekend is a three-night music festival held on campus. This page shows what the event will look and feel like.

Stage Concepts

A look at the three stages planned for APU Neon Weekend.

Main Stage

The Neon Arena

The main stage of the festival. It has a full LED lighting setup, a large sound system, and will host all three headline performances across the weekend.

Pulse Stage

The Pulse Deck

A smaller stage for live band performances. It is set up to feel closer to the crowd and will feature student bands and supporting acts during the event.

Echo Stage

The Echo Booth

A stage set up for DJ and electronic music sets. This stage runs late into the night and will have laser lighting and a visual effects display throughout.
