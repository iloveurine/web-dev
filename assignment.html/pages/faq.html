Frequently Asked Questions

What is this website about?
This website is for the "APU Neon Weekend" Event, providing information about the event schedules and the events being performed.

How do I create an account?
Click the Login button on the top right corner, this will bring you to the login screen. Select "Create account" and enter your credentials then select "Create Account" again.

How do I book a seat for a show?
Select the Events tab on the navigation screen, from there you can see the list of all events available. Click "View Details" to go to that event's page, then click "Book Seats". You will then be brought to the "Seat Booking" screen, where you can select the seat where you would like to sit at. After selecting the seat, select "Confirm Booking" and the page will update your booked seat in your Dashboard.

How do I view the event schedule for a specific day?
Click the drop down list where it says "All" in the Schedule page. Click it and select one of the days that are listed.

Is it possible to cancel a ticket after I've already bought one?
Please contact customer service at the Contact Us page, we will respond as soon as we can.
