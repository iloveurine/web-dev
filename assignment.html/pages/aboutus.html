The Vision & Concept

A youth-led, day-to-night neon festival transforming campus charity into an exciting, high-energy community experience.

Event Core Objective


APU NEON Weekend is a massive, high-energy charity festival hosted by Asia Pacific University that brings the campus to life for an incredible cause. During the day, the university transforms into a bustling carnival filled with food stalls, fun games, karaoke, and cool tech showcases where students can hang out and have fun. Once the sun sets, the real excitement begins as everyone puts on neon body paint, glow sticks, and bright t-shirts for a massive 5-kilometer night fun run. It is not a serious race at all; instead, it's a huge glowing party where around a thousand students walk, jog, and take photos through a route filled with music and bright light installations. Best of all, every single dollar raised from the weekend goes directly to local charities, like animal shelters and homes for the elderly, making it the ultimate weekend of having fun while doing good.
