Event Core Objective

APU Neon Weekend represents a purely imaginary student-led framework designed to bring vibrant artistic expressions and recreational showcases directly to the campus workspace. This platform encourages cross-faculty interaction by highlighting standard fine arts and modern live performance configurations.


By transforming traditional campus environments through dark canvas mediums and fluorescent theme layouts, participants experience an inclusive, stress-relieving creative escape.
