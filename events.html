APU Neon Weekend 2026

Festival
Events

Five events across three nights. Each one is a key part of APU Neon Weekend.

01
Night One · Friday

Opening Ceremony

▶ Friday 13 Nov · 18:00 · Neon Arena

The Opening Ceremony is the official start of APU Neon Weekend. It begins with a short welcome from the organising committee, followed by a LED light show and the first live performances of the weekend. This is the moment the campus officially becomes a festival ground.

  • Welcome address from the organising committee
  • Opening LED light show
  • First live performances of the weekend
  • Opening DJ set to warm up the crowd
02
Night One · Friday

Battle of the Bands

▶ Friday 13 Nov · 20:00 · Pulse Deck

Battle of the Bands gives APU student bands the chance to perform in front of the whole campus. Eight bands compete across two rounds. The audience votes for their favourite, and a panel of judges scores each performance. The winning band earns a spot to perform at the Sunday Closing Ceremony.

  • 8 student bands competing across two rounds
  • Live crowd voting after each performance
  • Judged by a panel of music professionals
  • Winner performs at the Sunday Closing Ceremony
03
Night Two · Saturday

Live Concert

▶ Saturday 14 Nov · 19:00 · Neon Arena

Saturday night is the main concert of APU Neon Weekend. The Neon Arena stage runs a full production with professional lighting, a multi-speaker sound system, and back-to-back performances from the festival's headline acts. Multiple artists perform throughout the night, ending with a confetti and pyrotechnics finale.

  • Full LED stage production with professional lighting
  • Multiple headline act performances
  • Special guest appearances
  • Pyrotechnics and confetti finale at the end of the night
04
Night Two · Saturday

DJ Night

▶ Saturday 14 Nov · 23:00 · Echo Booth

After the main concert ends, DJ Night takes over at the Echo Booth. Resident and guest DJs play sets covering electronic, house, and bass music until the early hours. The Echo Booth runs a laser and visual show throughout the night to go along with the music.

  • Resident and guest DJ sets
  • Music genres: electronic, house, and bass
  • Runs late into the night after the main concert
  • Laser and visual show throughout
05
Night Three · Sunday

Closing Ceremony

▶ Sunday 15 Nov · 22:00 · Neon Arena

The Closing Ceremony brings APU Neon Weekend to an end. The Sunday headline act performs on the Neon Arena stage, followed by a short set from the Battle of the Bands winner. The ceremony ends with a fireworks display visible from across the campus, along with official award presentations for the weekend's events.

  • Sunday headline act performance on the Neon Arena
  • Special set by the Battle of the Bands winner
  • Campus-wide fireworks display
  • Official award presentations for the weekend
