Why Attend APU Neon Weekend

One of the biggest student events of the year.

APU Neon Weekend is one of the biggest student events of the year. Over three nights, students can enjoy live music, exciting performances, great food, interactive activities, and a vibrant festival atmosphere. Whether you come for the artists, the food, or the experience, there is something for everyone.

Headline Performances

Three artists. Three nights. One stage.

Main Stage

Three Headline Performances

Get ready for unforgettable performances from Nova Eclipse, The Redline, and SOLARA. Each artist brings a different style of music to the festival, creating three nights of entertainment. From electronic beats and indie rock to R&B and neo-soul, visitors can experience a diverse range of performances on the Neon Arena stage.

Student Event

Battle of the Bands

Eight APU student bands compete across two rounds on Friday night. The audience votes, a panel of judges scores, and one band earns a spot at the Sunday Closing Ceremony.

Grand Finale

Closing Ceremony Fireworks

APU Neon Weekend ends on Sunday night with a fireworks display visible from across the campus. It follows the final headline performance and the Battle of the Bands winner's set.

More Than Just Music

Beyond the performances, there is plenty to see and do across all three nights.

Beyond the performances, APU Neon Weekend offers a wide range of activities. Explore the Neon Food Market, visit merchandise booths, enjoy the Gaming Arena, take photos at interactive photo booths, and participate in fun attractions across the festival grounds. The weekend is designed to create an exciting and memorable experience for all visitors.

Popular Activities

Some of the most visited spots at APU Neon Weekend outside of the stages.

01

Neon Food Market

Over 50 food stalls covering Malaysian street food and international options. The market runs from gates opening until the last performance ends each night.

02

Neon Gaming Arena

A free gaming zone with arcade machines, console setups, and nightly tournaments. Open to all festival-goers with a wristband across all three nights.

03

360 Spin Photo Booth

Step on the platform and record a slow-motion 360 video clip. The clip can be downloaded instantly and shared directly from the booth.

04

DJ Workshop

Walk-in sessions on professional DJ decks, guided by the festival's resident artists. No booking needed. Sessions run every hour throughout the weekend.

05

Live Art Wall

A large community mural that grows across all three nights. Anyone can pick up a spray can and add to it. No art experience is required.

06

Chill Lounge

A quiet area with hammocks, bean bags, and soft lighting. A good spot to rest and recharge between performances without leaving the festival grounds.

Festival Atmosphere

What it is actually like to be at APU Neon Weekend.

01

It Is on Your Campus

No travel needed. APU Neon Weekend takes place across the campus grounds, so everything is within walking distance. Stages, food, and activities are all close together.

02

Student-Run Event

APU Neon Weekend is organised and managed by APU students. The production, food market, activities, and performances are all put together by people on this campus.

03

Full Stage Production

The Neon Arena stage uses a professional LED rig, spatial audio, and pyrotechnics. It is a proper festival production built and operated on campus.

04

Food Available All Night

The Neon Food Market stays open until the last act finishes each night. With over 50 vendors, there is always something available no matter the time.

05

Campus Talent on Stage

Battle of the Bands puts APU student bands in front of the full crowd. It is one of the few events where you can watch people from your own campus perform live.

06

Activities All Weekend

Photo booths, the live art wall, DJ workshops, and the closing fireworks give everyone at the festival something to do and remember from the weekend.
