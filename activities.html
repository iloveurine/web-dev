Activities

APU Neon Weekend is more than just music. Food stalls, games, photo booths, and more are available across the festival grounds all three nights.

Street Food

Over 50 stalls serving food from across Malaysia and beyond.

Food Zone

The Neon Food Market

The Neon Food Market is open all three nights of the festival. More than 50 food vendors are set up in the food zone. You can find Malaysian street food, international options, and late-night snacks. The market opens when the gates open and stays open until the last performance ends each night.

  • Malaysian street food: nasi lemak, satay, lok lok, and more
  • International stalls: Korean BBQ, Japanese snacks, Western bites
  • Vegan and vegetarian options available throughout
  • Late-night food trucks open until close
  • Festival mocktail bars and fresh juice stalls

Gaming Area

A dedicated space to play games between sets.

Gaming Zone

The Neon Gaming Arena

The Neon Gaming Arena is open all three nights of the festival. It has classic arcade cabinets, retro console setups, and multiplayer gaming stations. All of them are free to use with your festival wristband. Tournaments run each night with prizes for the winners.

  • Classic arcade machines, free with your festival wristband
  • Console gaming setups available each night
  • Nightly multiplayer tournaments with prizes
  • Prize redemption counter on-site
